About Trelcore

Trusted expertise, built over decades

Trelcore Developments is a full-service construction management and building consultancy founded in Nassau, Bahamas — built by industry professionals with a combined track record spanning small-scale renovations to large, complex commercial builds.

"We work on buildings the way builders should — with accountability at every stage, not just at handover."

Our principals bring over 30 years of hands-on construction management experience to every engagement. We operate across Nassau and the greater Bahamas, with an established consulting presence in Ottawa, Ontario.
